Easy Overnight Oats

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/4 cup yogurt
  • 1 tablespoon chia seeds
  • 1 tablespoon honey
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt

Steps:

  1. Combine all the ingredients (besides the fruit) in a jar, seal, and shake.
  2. Open the jar and fold in the fruit.
  3. Seal the jar and refrigerate overnight.
